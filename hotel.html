Custom message with dynamic translation

Written by the guest — not a preset message.

Beyond preset messages, a guest can write freely in their language and the hotel can receive the message in its preferred operational language.

部屋の窓の近くから冷たい風が入ってきます。確認していただけますか。
Custom guest message: Cold air seems to be coming in near the room window. Could someone please check it?
Thank you for letting us know. We will ask maintenance to check the window area as soon as possible.
お知らせいただきありがとうございます。できるだけ早くメンテナンス担当に窓周辺を確認するよう依頼します。
