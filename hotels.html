Understand the guest journey without guessing.

SomaVio can use QR codes and stay links to help hotels understand how visitors interact with hotel information, official checks, local partners and offers — while keeping data privacy-conscious and consent-based.

Hotel QR code

A general QR code in the lobby, room, elevator, front desk or restaurant can open the Hotel Guest Hub. It can show which hotel/location generated the visit, language selected, pages opened, categories explored, saved ideas, official checks and common requests.

Aggregated, non-identifying unless the guest chooses to contact the hotel.

Individual guest link or QR

For pre-arrival emails, check-in or hotel messages, hotels can send a unique stay link or QR code. This can open a more personalized Hotel Guest Hub for the guest’s stay.

Clear consent, short-lived token, limited purpose, privacy notice and opt-in.

Partner / location QR code

Local partners can use their own QR codes in cafés, shuttles, spas, guides, shops, rentals, museums or promotional displays.

Aggregated source and engagement analytics, not hidden personal monitoring.

SomaVio should prioritize aggregated insights, clear consent, short-lived tokens, data minimization and guest control. Individual journey analysis should only happen when the guest has clearly consented through a Hotel Guest Hub or stay link.

Privacy-conscious stay-moment intelligence.
